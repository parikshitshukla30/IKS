About IKS

Indian Knowledge Systems encompass the diverse traditions of knowledge developed in India over millennia. These include philosophy, mathematics, astronomy, medicine, architecture, governance, arts, language, and ecology.

The concept has gained importance under India's National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, which encourages integrating traditional Indian knowledge with modern education.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is IKS?

    IKS (Indian Knowledge Systems) refers to the body of knowledge, practices, philosophies, sciences, arts, and technologies that developed in India over thousands of years.

    The concept has gained importance under India's National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, which encourages integrating traditional Indian knowledge with modern education.

    Why study IKS?

    To understand historical, cultural and intellectual contributions from India.

    Is IKS relevant today?

    Many concepts continue to influence education, wellness, sustainability and research.
